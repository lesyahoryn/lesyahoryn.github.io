Lesya Horyn

University of Chicago | horyn@uchicago.edu

I am a PhD student in particle physics on the ATLAS Experiment with the University of Chicago. Currently based at CERN, I work on FTK, an upgrade to the ATLAS trigger system and I search for long lived, metastable particles. I am passionnate about science communication and outreach. I am a student delegate to the AAAS Science and Human Rights Coalition and a executive committee member of the American Physical Society's Forum on Graduate Student Affairs. I am also an organizing committee member for Expanding Your Horizons Chicago. My website is under construction.
